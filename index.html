Singing With Dee

MORE THAN WHAT MEETS THE EYE

Haii, my name is Dina. I came to the world on the 5th of May. I have been living on this planet for 20 years now. I study applied arts in the GUC. I have been singing since i was six years old. Never been to a singing class in my life. My music taste is diverse, but my favorite genres are pop and R&B. I am obssesed with dogs, cars and ice cream. I've learned to appreciate all sorts of art and expression on my own. Fun fact, i am first in my family to be interested in such aspects. I am very spontaneous and i enjoy doing a ton of things. Along with singing, i love to dance. And as i mentioned that i study applied arts, so i love photography, videography and all that has to do with media design. But i also love creating posters and illustrating all the mess in my mind onto paper and digitalizing it. One of my favorite elements of design would be collage! I love to collect leaflets and maps and different magazines and take the time to make out crazy compositions from them.

A question i get asked a lot is why aren't my videos higher in quality? Why did it take me so long to actually create a singing page for myself? The answer to such questions would be.... I don't think i was ever going to iniate that page in the first place, or sign myself up for any sort of talent show. But, after being provoked by my friends (shotout to you guys; especially mariam and farah) i had to start posting. Because well, they had already taken the first step of creating the page. More and above , Mariam, being the craziest most spontaneous friend in my life, signed me up for a competition and sursprised me a few days before the audition. And despite the fact that i freaked out, I am wholeheartedly thankful to her irrational decisions because again, i would have never. I have no aim whatsoever from the page, i am grateful for all the followers i have and i feel responsibile to make them happy and i care not to let them down. But mainly, i sing for fun. I sing because i enjoy singing probably more than all my other hobbies and because it is my favorite way of expressing emotions.

I may be crazy annd i have a bunch of unnecessary theories about the world that may or may not intrigue you. You may not even care but curiosity will lead you. I believe a lot in karma. In the fact the what you put out in the world will someday return to you. In whatever form and whenever time. But it WILL return back to you eventually. Because, what goes around comes back around. Which brings us to circles, loops, spirals. Call'em what you wanna call'em. But they are how the world revolves. Literally! The Earth is round and it revolves around the circular sun in round orbits. The thoughts that get stuck in our heads and drown us are spirals of endless unanswered questions. If you think about it long enough you'll see my point of how circles are infinite just like our lives, our souls, our existence. Infinite like our emotions that lay within us, trauma, pain, hurt, love, joy, gratitude, excitement. We have no limit whatsoever to the capacity that we get to feel and the ideas we have to share, and that is what makes us limitless. Like spirals.
